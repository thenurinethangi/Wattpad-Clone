Hi, Wattpad Admins

Manage the world's largest storytelling community

Powerful tools to oversee users, stories, reports, and community interactions. Keep Wattpad safe and thriving with comprehensive administrative controls.

Manage Users

Oversee user accounts, handle verification processes, manage user permissions, and ensure community safety. Monitor user behavior, handle account issues, and maintain the integrity of the Wattpad community.

Manage Stories

Review and moderate story content, manage featured stories, handle content reports, and ensure all published content meets community guidelines. Control story visibility and organize content across different categories and genres.

Handle Reports

Process user reports efficiently, investigate content violations, manage dispute resolution, and maintain platform safety. Review flagged content, communicate with users, and take appropriate moderation actions to keep the community safe.

Announcements

Create and manage platform-wide announcements, schedule important notifications, communicate updates to the community, and broadcast critical information. Keep users informed about new features, policy changes, and community events.

Manage Genres

Organize and categorize content across different genres, manage featured content, curate trending stories, and maintain genre-specific guidelines. Ensure proper content classification and help readers discover stories that match their interests.

Transactions

Monitor platform transactions, manage premium subscriptions, oversee in-app purchases, handle payment disputes, and maintain financial records. Ensure secure payment processing and provide support for billing-related issues within the Wattpad ecosystem.
